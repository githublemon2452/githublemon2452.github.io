Preface

I want to improve my English pronunciation on a sudden impluse,so I purposefully start form the basic,the symbols.
I have learned the course from Alex’s videos from BBCLearningEnglsih.Here is the video link from bilibili.
【英音控的自我救赎】- 英音发音教程
This blog is some notes form Alex’s class.

Introduction

What do we mean by good pronunciation?
It’s fine to have a different accent,but what is important is you are able to speak clearly,and you don’t prevent others from understanding what you trying to say.
